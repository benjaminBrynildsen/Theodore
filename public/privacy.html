Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Theodore is an AI-assisted writing and audiobook tool operated by Benjamin Brynildsen ("Theodore," "we," "our," "us"). This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We try to be plain about it.

1. Information you give us

  • Account information. Email address, name, password (hashed), and any avatar image you upload. If you sign in with Google, we receive your email and name from Google as part of standard OAuth.
  • Project content. Text you write or that the app generates on your behalf — premises, chapters, scenes, character notes, cover images you upload, and audio renders generated for your projects. This is your work; we store it so you can come back to it.
  • Photos you choose to upload. If you upload a photo from your device library as a book cover, we store that image on our servers so it can be served back to you and shown alongside your project.
  • Voice input. If you use voice features (voice chat or Active Character mode), the audio is sent through speech-to-text and AI processing in real time to produce a response. We do not retain raw microphone recordings beyond the immediate processing.
  • Payment information. If you subscribe, payment processing is handled by Stripe. We never receive or store your card number; we only store identifiers Stripe gives us (customer ID, subscription ID, plan).
  • Support and outreach correspondence. If you contact us, we keep the message and our reply.

2. Information we collect automatically

  • Usage and analytics. Page views, button taps, generation events, IP address, approximate location derived from IP, browser/device type, and timestamps. Used to understand how the product is used and to debug issues.
  • Push notification tokens. If you grant permission on the mobile app, we store an Expo push token tied to your account so we can send notifications about your projects.
  • Cookies. A session cookie keeps you signed in. We do not use third-party advertising cookies on our own site.

3. How we use this information

  • To run the service: store and display your projects, generate text/audio when you ask, narrate your books, render covers, deliver audiobooks.
  • To process payments and manage your subscription.
  • To improve the product: spot bugs, prioritize features, identify common drop-off points.
  • To communicate with you: account notices, payment receipts, important changes to the service. Marketing emails only if you've opted in.
  • To protect the service from abuse, fraud, and security threats.

4. Third parties we share with

We use specific service providers because building everything in-house would be slow and worse for you. Each only sees the data necessary to do its job.

  • OpenAI (text generation and TTS narration) — receives the prompts and/or text we send for generation. Does not retain your prompts for training when accessed via the API.
  • xAI / Grok (text generation and voice features) — same scope as OpenAI.
  • ElevenLabs (audiobook narration and SFX) — receives the text we send for synthesis.
  • Stripe (payments) — handles your card data directly. We never see it.
  • Google (sign-in only, when you choose Google OAuth) — provides the email/name token; nothing else.
  • Render (hosting and database) — stores the data we describe in this policy on managed infrastructure in the United States.
  • Expo / Apple / Google FCM (push notification delivery) — receive only the notification payload and your device token, no project content.

We don't sell your data. We don't share your project content with third parties for advertising.

5. Data retention

  • Project content is kept for as long as your account exists. You can delete projects at any time inside the app.
  • Account data is kept while your account is active. If you delete your account, your projects, audio renders, and personal data are removed within 30 days, except where we are required to retain certain records for legal or accounting reasons (for example, payment records).
  • Logs and analytics are retained for up to 12 months and then deleted or aggregated.

6. Your rights

  • Access and correction. You can view and edit most of your information directly in the app's settings.
  • Deletion. Delete individual projects in the app, or email us to delete your entire account. Account deletion is irreversible.
  • Export. You can copy your prose out of the app at any time. If you'd like a bulk export, email us.
  • Opt-out of marketing. Every marketing email has an unsubscribe link.
  • If you're in California, the EU, or the UK, you have additional rights under CCPA / GDPR — we honor the same access, correction, deletion, and portability requests for everyone.

7. Children's privacy

Theodore is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, contact us and we'll delete it.

8. Security

Passwords are hashed. Connections to the service are encrypted in transit (HTTPS). Data at rest lives on Render's managed Postgres and persistent disks. No system is perfectly secure, but we follow standard practices and respond promptly to vulnerability reports.

9. International users

Theodore is operated from the United States, and the third-party services we use are mostly U.S.-based. By using Theodore from outside the U.S., you understand that your data is processed in the United States, where data-protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

10. Changes to this policy

If we make a material change, we'll update the "Last updated" date above and, when reasonable, notify you in the app or by email. Continued use after a change means you accept the updated policy.
